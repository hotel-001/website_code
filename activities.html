Discover Yercaud

Emerald Lake (Yercaud Lake)

A serene natural lake surrounded by lush hills, offering peaceful boating and calming views. Its emerald-green waters make it one of Yercaud’s most iconic and relaxing attractions.

Pagoda Point

A breathtaking viewpoint overlooking the valleys and Salem cityscape. The cool breeze and panoramic views make it a perfect spot for sunrise and sunset moments.

Kiliyur Falls

A stunning waterfall hidden within dense forest trails, ideal for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The cascading waters create a refreshing, picturesque escape.

Botanical Garden

A vibrant garden showcasing rare plants, colorful blooms, and curated landscape spaces. It’s a peaceful retreat for photography, walks, and nature appreciation.

Shevaroy Hills

A high-altitude haven with misty peaks, cool climate, and dense forest surroundings. Known for its scenic beauty and tranquillity, it offers a rejuvenating experience away from the city.
