VAMSI'S WOODSIDE

Experience an extraordinary stay at VAMSI'S WOODSIDE in peaceful 'Lake Forest' – Yercaud.

This charming three-bedroom villa lies amidst serene coffee estates, crafted with beautiful wooden décor and adorned balconies—perfect for a cup of coffee, a laugh with friends, or a quiet moment with nature. Situated 10 km from Yercaud Lake on way to Muluvi (via Nagaloor), it offers cool breezes, captivating views, and sometimes, friendly bison sightings from your balcony.

Designed with elegance and care, each corner reflects balance—of style, warmth, and wilderness. Whether you're on a family holiday, a retreat with friends, or working remotely amidst nature, villa ensures an experience of privacy, peace, and comfort.

• Fully equipped kitchen (fridge, stove, microwave, cutlery, cookware)

Signature Experience: Our unique "Balcony Theatre," where you can enjoy movies or matches under the stars—a perfect harmony of entertainment and nature.
