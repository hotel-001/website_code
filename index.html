House Rules & Policies

To ensure a pleasant and comfortable stay for all our guests, we kindly ask you to adhere to the following guidelines.

  • Check-in/Check-out: Please inquire for timings. Guests are liable for any damages to the property.
  • Pets: To maintain our standards, we regret that pets are not allowed on the property.
  • Generator: The generator is provided for basic power needs only.
  • Projector & Screen: The projector is available until 10:00 PM and must be run on mains power. The screen retracts automatically.
  • Noise Level: Please keep noise to a minimum after 10:00 PM to respect the wildlife and our neighbors.
  • Speakers & Karaoke: Portable speakers are for balcony use only. Karaoke is restricted to the main hall area.
  • Games: Children's cricket sets are available. All other games must be returned to the caretaker upon departure.

Rent for drivers’ quarters is Rs. 500 per day; Campfire will be provided on demand at additional Rs.1000 each time.
